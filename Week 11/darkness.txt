In an instant the lodge was filled with darkness. It wasn't just an
absence of light, but a fullness of dark. A smothering, uncompromising,
deep black occupied every empty space. There were no stars, no glowing orb,
no nacreous, shimmering clouds. A hand brought in front of the eyes could
not be seen. There was no dimension, no shadow, no silhouette of black on
black. The sense of sight had lost all value.
